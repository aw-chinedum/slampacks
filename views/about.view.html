About Slampacks

This is a digital trading card pack opening website where you can open custom packs containing a single card. That card is then sold to our site and you can then purchase the card or another card of equal or lesser value, and have it sent to you (some restrictions apply) or use the trade-in value of the card to open more packs, or both.

When you click on a card it will show you the odds of pulling that card out of it's pack, as well as the option to purchase the card. When purchasing a pack the value of the card you pulled will be added to your account. The idea for this site is to give people a better chance at getting the cards they actually want to have. Available inventory may result in your purchase of a card being refunded as well as price fluctuations.
